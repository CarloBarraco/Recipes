#Ramen
![ramen](ramen.jpg)
##Ingredients
###Salted pork
* 1 lb pork
* 1 tsp salt

###Soup
* 6 cups water (1.5L)
* 50g ginger root, sliced
* 3 cloves garlic, skinned
* 1 bunch green onions
* 4 Tbsp soy sauce
* 2 Tbsp sake
* 1 tsp salt
* 1 tsp sesame oil

###Noodles
* 9 oz fresh angel hair pasta (225g)
* 8 cups water (2L)
* 2 Tbsp baking soda

###Topping
* Boiled egg halves
* Bean sprouts, blanched briefly
* Green onions, cut finely
